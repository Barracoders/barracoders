Tripwire

Tripwire is a powerful and innovative app that provides users with the ability to detect criminals and sex offenders in their local area. Through a user-friendly interface, tripwire gives you all the information you need to make informed decisions about your safety. You can easily view an offender's photo, address, and crime history. Made by Josh Worthington for 2023 BHS Innovation Competition
