Resources

Our Resources

Glitch

Glitch.com is a user-friendly platform that empowers creators to build, collaborate on, and host web applications and projects effortlessly. Offering a seamless experience for both beginners and seasoned developers, Glitch.com provides a range of tools and templates to jumpstart projects, along with a community-driven ethos that encourages sharing and learning. Whether you're prototyping a new idea, learning to code, or collaborating with others, Glitch.com makes coding accessible and enjoyable.

FreeCodeCamp

FreeCodeCamp.com is an interactive learning platform that offers free coding tutorials and certifications in web development, data science, and more. Designed for learners of all levels, FreeCodeCamp.com provides hands-on coding challenges and projects to help users build practical skills. With a supportive community and a commitment to making technology education accessible to everyone, FreeCodeCamp.com is a go-to resource for aspiring developers looking to enhance their coding abilities and advance their careers in tech.

SoloLearn

SoloLearn is a mobile and web-based platform that offers free interactive courses in coding and computer science topics. Ideal for beginners and enthusiasts alike, SoloLearn provides bite-sized lessons, quizzes, and coding challenges to help users learn and practice coding on the go. With a vibrant community and opportunities to earn certifications, SoloLearn makes it easy and enjoyable to master programming languages like Python, JavaScript, and more, right from your smartphone or computer.

Scratch

Scratch is a beginner-friendly programming language and online community where users can create interactive stories, games, and animations. Designed primarily for children and beginners, Scratch uses a visual programming interface that allows users to drag and drop code blocks to create projects without needing to write traditional code. With a focus on creativity and learning through exploration, Scratch encourages users to experiment and collaborate in a supportive online environment, making it an excellent introduction to the world of coding and computer science.

CodeHS

CodeHS is an online platform that provides comprehensive computer science and programming courses for schools and individual learners. Offering a structured curriculum from introductory to advanced levels, CodeHS features interactive lessons, coding exercises, and projects in languages like Python, Java, and JavaScript. With a focus on both learning fundamentals and real-world application, CodeHS supports educators in teaching computer science effectively while offering students hands-on experience to develop their coding skills and prepare for future careers in technology.

Ozaria

Ozaria is an educational platform that combines storytelling with game-based learning to teach computer science and coding concepts. Designed for middle school students, Ozaria takes learners on an interactive journey through a fantasy world where they solve puzzles and complete quests using coding skills. With engaging characters and a curriculum aligned with educational standards, Ozaria aims to make learning coding both fun and accessible, empowering students to develop problem-solving abilities and computational thinking in an immersive and engaging environment.
